Ordinal Regression Model Analysis


An ordinal regression model allows a user to take ranked data and find meaning in the order rather than ignore it. We used this type of model to predict medal chances of Olympic winners from both winter and summer Olympics. Using data from 1964 to 2016 to train the dataset, we predicted the chances of winning gold for known gold medal winners from PyeongChang 2018 and Tokyo 2020. Our results show a wide range of probabilities, which shows Olympic winners are never guaranteed and must always beat the odds to win!
